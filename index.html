Frequently Asked Questions

Which areas in Zimbabwe do you cover?

We're based in Harare and Mvurwi and install fencing, gates and steel structures nationwide — residential, commercial, industrial and farm sites across Zimbabwe.

Do you offer free quotations?

Yes. We provide free, no-obligation site assessments and quotes. Call 0714 194 324 or 0783 394 176, or send the quote form and we'll reply on WhatsApp.

Do you supply materials as well as install?

We do both. RhinoFence manufactures and supplies fencing materials and gates, and our own teams handle the full installation — so quality and timelines stay under our control.

What's the best fence for security?

It depends on your site and budget. Clear view (358 anti-climb mesh) and palisade offer strong physical deterrence; add electric fencing and razor wire for an energised, alarmed layer. We'll recommend the right combination during your free assessment.

Can you automate my gate?

Yes. We fabricate custom sliding and swing gates and can fit automation with remote and keypad access. Just mention it when you request your quote.
