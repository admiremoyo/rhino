Welding & Steel Works in Zimbabwe

General fabrication, on-site welding and repairs — from balustrades and burglar bars to bespoke metalwork, done properly.

Not every job is a fence. RhinoFence's welding and steel-works division handles the everyday and the custom — security bars, balustrades, brackets, repairs and one-off fabrication — with the same standard of workmanship we put into our fencing.

Based in Harare and Mvurwi and working across Zimbabwe, we offer workshop fabrication and on-site welding for homes, businesses and industry.

Benefits of Welding & Steel Works

Versatile Fabrication

Burglar bars, gates, grilles, balustrades, brackets, stands and custom pieces — if it's steel, we can make it.

On-Site Welding

Mobile welding for repairs and installations where the work can't come to the workshop.

Quality Craftsmanship

Neat, strong welds and a proper finish — work you'll be happy to show off.

Repairs & Modifications

We fix, reinforce and modify existing steelwork, gates and structures.

Welding & Steel Works FAQs

Do you do small welding jobs and repairs?

Yes. No job is too small — from a single repair or set of burglar bars to bespoke fabrication, we're happy to help.

Can you weld on site?

Yes. We offer mobile on-site welding for repairs and installations that can't be done in the workshop.
