Diamond mesh (chain-link) fencing is the practical, budget-friendly way to define and secure a boundary. It's quick to install, lets light and air through, and suits everything from residential yards to large sports and industrial enclosures.

RhinoFence supplies and installs galvanised and PVC-coated diamond mesh across Zimbabwe in a range of heights and wire gauges, on steel or concrete posts to suit your site.

The most economical way to enclose large areas without sacrificing durability.

Diamond Mesh Fence FAQs

What's the difference between galvanised and PVC-coated mesh?

Galvanised mesh is zinc-coated for rust resistance and best value. PVC-coated adds a plastic layer for extra weather protection and a tidy green finish — ideal for schools and homes.

How tall can diamond mesh fencing go?

Commonly 1.2 m to 3.0 m. Taller runs for sports grounds and security sites are no problem with the right posts and straining.
