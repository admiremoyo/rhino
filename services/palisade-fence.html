Palisade Fence in Zimbabwe

Rugged, cost-effective steel palisade fencing with pointed pales — a proven, high-deterrent boundary for industrial, commercial and residential sites.

Get a Free Quote Chat on WhatsApp

Steel palisade fencing is one of the most popular and economical security solutions in Zimbabwe. Its vertical pales with spiked or splayed tops are hard to climb and highly visible, sending a clear message to would-be intruders while standing up to years of hard use.

RhinoFence fabricates and installs palisade fencing to order — choosing pale profile, height and finish to match your security needs and budget, from single-property boundaries to large factory perimeters.

Benefits of Palisade Fence

Excellent Value

Palisade delivers strong physical security at a lower cost per metre than most alternatives — ideal for long industrial and farm boundaries.

High Deterrence

Pointed or splayed pale tops and a tall, rigid structure make climbing difficult and obvious.

Tough & Long-Lasting

Heavy-gauge steel, galvanised and optionally powder-coated, resists corrosion and impact.

Low Maintenance

No gaps for debris, easy to inspect, and simple to repair pale-by-pale if ever damaged.

Finishes & options

Choose from ‘W’ or ‘D’ section pales, single or triple-pointed tops, and heights typically from 1.8 m to 3.0 m. Add an electric-fence top or razor coil for an extra layer of protection.

  • Schools & institutions

Palisade Fence FAQs

Is palisade cheaper than clear view fencing?

Generally yes — palisade is very cost-effective for long runs, which is why it's popular for industrial and farm perimeters. Clear view offers better anti-climb performance and looks; we'll help you choose.

Can palisade be powder-coated in a colour?

Yes. We galvanise for rust protection and can powder-coat in your chosen colour for a smart, durable finish.
